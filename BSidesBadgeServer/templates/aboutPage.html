About the Badges

All three badges presented at BSides Cape Town 2016

From left to right, White ( Attendee ), Blue (Speaker/presenter/organiser), Black (Badge Staff)

Badge Interface

Badge Interface

The badge has the following components
  • Buttons
  • Screen
  • Infrared Receiver and Transmitter
  • ESP8266 (processor and WiFi)
  • 600mah Lipo Battery
  • USB Charging

Badge Screens

Different Screens on the badges


The badges themselves offer 5 different screens that are available listed below as well as playing The Game. Below lists what each badge has.

Logo Screen



This screen simply shows the BSides Cape Town 2016 display.

Badge/About Screen


This screen lists the details about your badge, including the following:

* Number of challenges solved.
* The code used to change the alias
* The Badge ID also used on the alias page


Challenge Screen



This screen lists the challenges that you have completed as well as give you a chance to "play" the prize if available.

To use this screen use the keypad on the right (A,B,C,D buttons) to move left and right (A and D) and when you wish to play the 'prize' you can press up (B).
The prizes could be one of the following:

* Warbadging (scan available WiFi networks)
* Pong ( 2 players on one badge )
* Skeleton Dance
* Alias Waterfall

TO QUIT CHALLENGES PLEASE PRESS TO TWO INNER BUTTONS ( LEFT and A)


Rock, Paper, Scissors, Spock, Lizard



This screen is the Rock, Paper, Scissors, Spock, Lizard. To find out about this game you can click here.

To play this game do the following:
1. Make your selection using the A and D keys between Rock, Paper, Scissors, Spock, Lizard
2. Press the B to start searching for players
3. Put your badge near another badge that is also searching for players
4. When your selection list shows additional badges use the B and C keys to select your opponent ( Note this will be the opponents Badge ID)
5. Win or Lose!


TO QUIT THIS GAME PLEASE PRESS TO TWO INNER BUTTONS ( LEFT and A)

Schedule


The Schedule screen shows.... the schedule.
To navigate this screen use the A and D keys.
